About Consultations

Security is situational.
An initial consultation is a strategy session. Not a sales pitch.

During our time together we will:

  • Identify your most pressing security and privacy concerns
  • Clarify your threat landscape (technical, interpersonal, organizational)
  • Prioritize realistic, high-impact protections
  • Outline immediate and long-term action steps

You'll leave with more clarity, and a plan for how to move forward.

What to Expect

Duration: 30, 60, or 90 minutes
Format: Virtual (Signal, Zoom, Jitsi, ect.)
Confidentiality: Discretion and privacy are prioritized

Financial resources should never present a barrier to safety. Therefore, all services are available on a sliding scale.
