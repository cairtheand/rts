RowanTree Security and Privacy

RowanTree provides accessible, compassionate cybersecurity and privacy services to those making the world a better place. Whether you are a nonprofit, an activist, a healthcare provider, or someone facing a personal digital threat, we are here to protect and empower you.

What We Do

  • Privacy and security education tailored for at-risk professionals and communities
  • Preventative cybersecurity consulting for nonprofits and volunteer groups
  • Crisis response for individuals facing harassment, doxxing, or stalking
  • Online data removal
  • Secure communication setup and onboarding
  • Platform-specific guidance, optimization, troubleshooting
  • Digital security guide authoring and editing
  • Personalized security and privacy assessments

Who We Serve

RowanTree strengthens the security posture of organizations advancing social justice, and protects the privacy, dignity, and safety of activists, survivors, professionals, and targeted groups.

  • Nonprofits and Volunteer Groups Working to Advance Social Justice
  • Individuals Experiencing or Vulnerable to Doxxing and/or Digital Harassment
  • Advocates, Activists, and Plaintiffs
  • LGBTQ+ Community Members
  • Survivors of Intimate Partner Violence and Stalking
  • Researchers, Professors, Therapists, Doctors, Lawyers
  • Students
  • Sex Workers
  • People practicing alternative relationship structures (ie: Non-monogamy, Sugar, Kink)

How We Do Things

Our goal is for you to live, work, and advocate bravely.
We prioritize making expertise accessible, compassionate support, and building knowledge together.

Financial resources should never present a barrier to digital safety. Therefore, all services are available on a sliding scale.
