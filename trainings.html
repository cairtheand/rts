RowanTree Security and Privacy

RowanTree provides accessible, compassionate cybersecurity and privacy services to those making the world a better place. Whether you are a nonprofit, an activist, a healthcare provider, or someone facing a personal digital threat, we are here to protect and empower you.

Personalized Trainings

RowanTree offers interactive workshops and training sessions designed around the unique needs of your organization. We take the time to understand your priorities and challenges, then tailor sessions to fit your mission and your people. Participants walk away empowered with practical skills and strategies to improve digital security.

  • Social Media Safety for Nonprofits
  • Doxxing Prevention for Therapists
  • Signal 101 and 102
  • What To Do About Mercenary Spyware
  • Keys to Security: Password Managers & MFA Made Simple
